Europe's Energy Conundrum

Recently, there has been a great deal of concern about the price and availability of energy in the Europe. Most European import significant quantities of their eneregy needs, hence there markets are subject to instability abroad. Of particular concern is the recent conflict in Ukraine, as Russia is a major supply of energy (particualarly natural gas) to Europe.
